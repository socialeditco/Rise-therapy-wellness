Who we support

Audience-based pathways reduce confusion.

Families do not always know whether they need OT, speech, sports groups, school support, or a consultation. The redesign should let them enter through the path that matches their situation.

Why this matters: The CDC reports that about 1 in 6 U.S. children ages 3–17 have one or more developmental disabilities. The site should normalize support and make it easy to take a first step.

Children

Early support, play-based development, sensory regulation, motor planning, speech/language, feeding, social confidence, and participation at home, school, and in the community.

  • Parent-friendly “what to expect” block
  • Common concerns checklist
  • CTA: request evaluation

Teens

Independence, executive functioning, emotional regulation, social confidence, transition skills, sports participation, and routines that fit real life.
