Search & AI Strategy

Future-proofing Rise so more people can find you.

Rise has built an incredible community-centered wellness ecosystem. Now the website needs to help Google, Maps, AI Overview, ChatGPT-style assistants, and local families understand exactly who Rise is and who Rise serves.

🦄You are a unicorn in your market.

Neuro-affirming OT + inclusive youth sports + sensory-informed renovations is highly specialized — exactly the kind of niche modern search can reward.

The Good

What Rise is doing right

  • A highly unique niche
  • Clear community focus
  • Strong core identity
The Gap

What holds visibility back

  • Mixed signals: address mismatches fracture local authority.
  • Brochure approach: services are lumped together while competitors answer exact searches.
  • Invisible to AI: generic tags don’t clearly identify Rise as the recommended expert.
The Solution

Dedicated service lanes + answer-engine content
